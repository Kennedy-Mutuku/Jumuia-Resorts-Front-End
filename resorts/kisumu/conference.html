Conference Policies

Important information for planning your event

Child Policy

Children below 3 years sharing the same room with parents: No extra cost
Children between 3–12 years sharing the same room with 1 or 2 adults: 50% per child of the payable room rate (Maximum 2 children)
Children between 3–12 years in their own room: 75% per child of the payable room rate (Maximum 2 children)

Day Room Policy

Room held after 12 noon to 6 pm: 75% of your rate
Room held after 6 pm: Full rate

Extra Bed & Person Policy

Extra bed for adults: 40% of the twin/double room rate
Extra person in a room: KSh 2,000.00
Food supplement per person: KSh 1,000.00
