Important Policies

Please review our policies before booking

Child Policy

  • Children below 3 years: Free (Sharing with parents)
  • Children 3-12 years sharing with parents: 50% of adult rate
  • Children 3-12 years in own room: 75% of adult rate
  • Maximum 2 children per room

Check-in/Check-out

  • Check-in time: 2:00 PM
  • Check-out time: 11:00 AM
  • Day room (12pm-6pm): 75% of room rate
  • Late check-out (after 6pm): Full night's rate

Extra Bed & Person Policy

  • Extra bed for adults: 40% of twin/double room rate
  • Extra person in a room: KES 2,000.00
  • Food supplement per person: KES 1,000.00
  • Swimming pool access: Separate charges apply

General Notes

  • Rates inclusive of statutory taxes
  • Rates subject to change without notice
  • Special rates for groups and conferences
  • Local excursions charged separately
