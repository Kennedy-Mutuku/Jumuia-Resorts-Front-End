Important Policies

Please review our policies before booking

Child Policy

  • Children 4 years & below: Free when sharing room with parents
  • Children 5-11 years sharing with parents: 50% of adult rate
  • Children 5-11 years exclusive use of room: 75% of adult rate
  • Extra bed for adults: 40% of room rate

Check-in/Check-out

  • Check-in time: 12:00 PM (Noon)
  • Check-out time: 10:00 AM
  • Day room charges (12pm-6pm): 75% of room rate
  • Late check-out (after 6pm): Full night's rate

Meal Plans

  • Bed & Breakfast: Room + Breakfast
  • Half Board: Room + Breakfast + Dinner
  • Full Board: Room + Breakfast + Lunch + Dinner
  • Open Restaurant: Available for additional meals

General Notes

  • Rates are inclusive of statutory taxes
  • Rates are subject to change without notice
  • All bookings require deposit confirmation
  • Special church and corporate rates available
