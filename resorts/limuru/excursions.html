Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our excursions and activities

What should I wear for excursions?

+

Comfortable walking shoes, layered clothing for changing weather, hat, sunglasses, and sunscreen. For hiking, proper hiking boots are recommended. We'll provide specific recommendations based on your chosen excursion.

Are excursions suitable for children?

+

Yes! Many of our excursions are family-friendly. We have specific activities suitable for different age groups. Please inquire about age recommendations when booking. Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult.

What happens in case of bad weather?

+

We monitor weather conditions closely. If an excursion needs to be canceled due to unsafe weather, we will offer to reschedule or provide a full refund. Some excursions may proceed with appropriate rain gear.

Are meals included in excursion packages?

+

Most half-day excursions include water and snacks. Full-day excursions include packed lunch. Day trips include all meals as specified. Please check individual excursion descriptions for specific inclusions.

What is your cancellation policy?

+

Cancellations made 48 hours or more before the excursion receive a full refund. Cancellations 24-48 hours before receive 50% refund. Less than 24 hours notice or no-shows are non-refundable. Weather-related cancellations are fully refundable.

Can I book private excursions?

+

Yes! Private excursions are available for all our activities. These can be customized to your specific interests, schedule, and group size. Private excursions typically cost 30-50% more than group rates depending on group size.
