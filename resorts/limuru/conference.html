Conference & Meeting Facilities

State-of-the-art conference facilities in the serene Limuru highlands. Perfect for corporate meetings, workshops, church events, and special gatherings.

Ideal Conference Destination

Our conference center is specifically designed to provide a conducive environment for productive meetings and events. Located in the tranquil Limuru highlands, away from city distractions, we offer the perfect setting for focused discussions and creative thinking.

  • Multiple Conference Rooms: Facilities for 20 to 500 delegates
  • Modern Equipment: High-speed internet, projectors, sound systems
  • Flexible Setup: Theater, classroom, boardroom, or banquet style
  • Professional Support: Dedicated conference coordinators
