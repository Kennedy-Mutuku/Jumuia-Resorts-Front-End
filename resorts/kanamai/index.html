Welcome to Kanamai Beach Resort

Nestled along the pristine shores of the Kenyan coast, our beach resort offers the perfect blend of tropical relaxation and professional conference facilities. With direct access to white sandy beaches and crystal-clear waters, we provide an ideal setting for both business retreats and family vacations.

  • Private Beach Access: Direct access to pristine sandy beaches with sun loungers and beach service
  • Conference Facilities: State-of-the-art meeting rooms for 20-500 people with ocean views
  • Water Sports Center: Snorkeling, boat rides, and other exciting water activities
  • Freshwater Pool: Large swimming pool with separate children's section and pool bar

Our Services & Amenities

Everything you need for a comfortable and productive coastal stay

Conference Facilities

Modern conference halls and meeting rooms with ocean views, equipped with audiovisual systems for corporate meetings, workshops, and large events.

Accommodation

Comfortable rooms ranging from standard to ocean-front suites, plus budget-friendly hostel accommodation for groups.

Beachfront Dining

Delicious local seafood and international cuisine served in our scenic restaurant with ocean views or right on the beach.

Water Sports

Beach Service
