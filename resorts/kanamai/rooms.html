Important Policies

Please review our policies before booking

Child Policy

  • Children 3 years & below: Free (Sharing with parents)
  • Children 4-11 years sharing with parents: 50% of adult rate
  • Children 4-11 years in own room: 75% of adult rate
  • Extra Bed for adults: 40% of room rate

Check-in/Check-out

  • Check-in time: 12:00 PM (Noon)
  • Check-out time: 10:00 AM
  • Day room charges (12pm-6pm): 75% of room rate
  • Late check-out (after 6pm): Full night's rate

Cancellation Policy

  • 30-20 days before: 15% of booking value
  • 20-10 days before: 30% of booking value
  • 10-0 days before: 100% of booking value
  • No Show: 100% of booking value

General Notes

  • Rates inclusive of statutory taxes
  • Rates subject to change without notice
  • Team building trainer available separately
  • Local attractions charged separately
