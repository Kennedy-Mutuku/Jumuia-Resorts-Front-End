Our Story

Jumuia Resorts is a chain of Christian-oriented, medium-sized hotels in Kenya managed by the National Council of Churches of Kenya (NCCK). For decades, we have been providing tranquil environments for business travelers, conferences, weddings, and family vacations across multiple locations in Kenya.

"Hospitality with a Christian touch" isn't just our motto—it's the foundation of everything we do. We believe in serving with compassion, integrity, and excellence in every interaction.

Our resorts are strategically located to offer both serene getaways and convenient business solutions, each with its unique character and charm. From beachfront paradises to highland retreats and urban hubs, we provide diverse experiences united by our commitment to exceptional service.
