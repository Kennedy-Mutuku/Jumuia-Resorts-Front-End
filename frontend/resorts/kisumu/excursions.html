Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our Kisumu excursions and activities

What should I wear for Lake Victoria excursions?

+

Light, comfortable clothing, hat, sunglasses, and sunscreen. For boat trips, bring a light jacket as it can be windy on the lake. Comfortable walking shoes for land tours.

Are excursions suitable for children?

+

Yes! Many of our excursions are family-friendly. Impala Sanctuary, Kisumu Museum, and boat rides are particularly popular with children. Age recommendations vary by activity.

What happens in case of bad weather?

+

For water activities, we monitor weather and lake conditions. If an excursion needs to be canceled due to unsafe conditions, we will offer to reschedule or provide a full refund.

Are meals included in excursion packages?

+

Most full-day excursions include lunch (typically local cuisine or fresh fish). Half-day excursions include water and snacks. Please check individual excursion descriptions for specific inclusions.

What is your cancellation policy?

+

Cancellations made 24 hours or more before the excursion receive a full refund. Cancellations less than 24 hours before receive 50% refund. No-shows are non-refundable. Weather-related cancellations are fully refundable.

Can we customize our own tour itinerary?

+

Absolutely! We specialize in creating personalized Lake Victoria experiences. Contact our excursion desk to discuss combining multiple activities or creating a custom itinerary for your group.
