Welcome to Limuru Country Home

Nestled in the scenic Limuru highlands, our country home offers a perfect blend of tranquility and modern amenities. Surrounded by lush tea plantations and breathtaking views of the Great Rift Valley, we provide an ideal setting for conferences, Religious retreats, family vacations, and special events.

  • Perfect for Religious Events: Special packages for prayer retreats, conferences, and youth camps
  • Conference Facilities: State-of-the-art meeting rooms for 20-600 people
  • Budget-Friendly Hostels: Affordable accommodation for large groups and students
  • Nature Immersion: Guided nature walks through tea plantations and scenic viewpoints
