Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our coastal excursions and activities

What should I wear for coastal excursions?

+

Light, comfortable clothing, swimwear under clothes for water activities, hat, sunglasses, and sunscreen. For cultural tours, modest clothing is recommended. Water shoes are useful for reef walks.

Are excursions suitable for children?

+

Yes! Many of our excursions are family-friendly. Beach walks, Haller Park, Wild Waters Park, and Mamba Village are particularly popular with children. Age recommendations vary by activity.

What happens in case of bad weather?

+

For water activities, we monitor weather and sea conditions. If an excursion needs to be canceled due to unsafe conditions, we will offer to reschedule or provide a full refund.

Are meals included in excursion packages?

+

Most full-day excursions include lunch (typically seafood or local cuisine). Half-day excursions include water and snacks. Please check individual excursion descriptions for specific inclusions.

What is your cancellation policy?

+

Cancellations made 24 hours or more before the excursion receive a full refund. Cancellations less than 24 hours before receive 50% refund. No-shows are non-refundable. Weather-related cancellations are fully refundable.

Can we customize our own tour itinerary?

+

Absolutely! We specialize in creating personalized coastal experiences. Contact our excursion desk to discuss combining multiple activities or creating a custom itinerary for your group.
