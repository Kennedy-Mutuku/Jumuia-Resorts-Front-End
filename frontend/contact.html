Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our resorts and services

What are your check-in and check-out times?

Check-in time is 2:00 PM and check-out time is 11:00 AM at all our resorts. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request and subject to availability.

Do you offer airport transfer services?

Yes, we offer airport transfer services at an additional cost. Please contact the resort directly at least 24 hours before your arrival to arrange airport pickup.

Are your resorts child-friendly?

Absolutely! All our resorts are family-friendly. We offer family rooms, children's menus, and safe recreational facilities. Children under 5 years stay free when sharing with parents.

What conference facilities do you offer?

We offer state-of-the-art conference facilities with capacities ranging from 20 to 400 guests. All venues include audio-visual equipment, high-speed internet, and professional support staff.
